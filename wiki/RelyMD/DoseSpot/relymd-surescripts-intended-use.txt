What is the purpose and specific intended use case(s) for accessing, exchanging, or using Surescripts data?

RelyMD is a telehealth platform where licensed providers (MD, DO, NP, PA) see patients in virtual visits. We are integrating DoseSpot into our provider application so that, during or right after a visit, the rendering provider can electronically send a prescription to the patient's pharmacy. DoseSpot is our certified intermediary to Surescripts — RelyMD does not connect to Surescripts directly.

All Surescripts data accessed through DoseSpot is used solely for treatment of an established RelyMD patient by the credentialed RelyMD provider who conducted that patient's visit. Specifically, we use it to:

1. Sync patient demographics (name, DOB, gender, address, mobile phone, height, weight) into DoseSpot so a prescription can be tied to the correct patient.
2. Exchange allergies and the patient's self-reported medication list to support safe-prescribing checks.
3. Search the drug catalog so the provider can pick the correct medication, strength, form, and dispense unit.
4. Look up the patient's pharmacy (retail or mail-order) from the Surescripts pharmacy directory.
5. Transmit new prescriptions through Surescripts to the chosen pharmacy for dispensing.
6. Read back the resulting prescription record so it appears on the patient's chart for continuity of care.

Scope of this integration: new prescriptions only (no refill requests, RxRenewal, or RxChange), and non-controlled substances only (EPCS / controlled substances are explicitly disabled at medication selection). Prescribing is limited to the licensed provider who conducted the visit, in states where that provider holds an active license.
