Interview skill (similar to `grill-me`) focused on shipping features that feel finished. Walks through 10 UX dimensions one question at a time, then produces a grouped, severity-tagged polish checklist.

Invoke with `/ux-polish <feature or screen>`. Adapts questions to platform (mobile/web/CLI). Offers `/to-issues` handoff at the end.
